What is FrankPass?

FrankPass is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you generate strong, random passwords for all your accounts and applications. With the increasing risks of cyber threats, having unique and complex passwords is essential for protecting your digital life. FrankPass makes password creation effortless and reliable.

Features

  • Truly Random Passwords: FrankPass uses cryptographically secure algorithms to generate passwords that are hard to guess and resistant to brute-force attacks.
  • Customizable Options: Choose password length, include/exclude symbols, numbers, uppercase, and lowercase letters.
  • Instant Generation: Generate a new password with a single click.
  • Privacy First: All passwords are generated locally in your browserâ€”nothing is ever stored or sent to a server.

Why Use FrankPass?

  • Security: Weak and reused passwords are a major cause of online breaches. FrankPass ensures your passwords are both unique and strong.
  • Simplicity: No signup required. No ads. No clutter. Just copy and use your new password.
  • Open Source: FrankPass is open source, so you can review the code and contribute to its improvement.

How to Use

  1. Select your desired password length and character options.
  2. Click the Generate Password button.
  3. Copy your new secure password and use it wherever you need.
